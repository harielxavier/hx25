==============================================
HANDLING NEGATIVE REVIEWS
==============================================

IF you get a negative review (hopefully never!):

STEP 1: Don't Panic
- Take 24 hours before responding
- Don't respond emotionally

STEP 2: Reach Out Privately First
- Call or email the client
- Ask what went wrong
- Offer to make it right

STEP 3: Public Response (After Private Resolution)
"Hi [Name], thank you for your feedback. I'm sorry we didn't meet your expectations. I've reached out to you privately to discuss how we can make this right. Client satisfaction is our top priority."

STEP 4: Request Removal or Update
- If you resolve the issue, politely ask if they'd update/remove review
- Google allows review removal if: fake, spam, conflict of interest, or sexually explicit content

STEP 5: Bury It With Positive Reviews
- Double down on review requests from happy clients
- New positive reviews will push negative one down
